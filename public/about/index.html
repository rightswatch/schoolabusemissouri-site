About This Site

This website is published by Rights Watch Media Group LLC, an independent media organization that publishes authoritative legal information resources for Missouri and Illinois residents.

Our Mission

Rights Watch Media Group LLC publishes informational websites covering areas of law that significantly affect Missouri and Illinois families — including mesothelioma and asbestos disease, school sexual abuse and Title IX, and institutional accountability.

Our goal is to provide accurate, accessible, plain-language information so that survivors, patients, families, and advocates can understand their legal rights and make informed decisions.

What This Site Is

This is an informational resource — not a law firm website, not a lead-generation service, and not a substitute for legal advice. We do not represent clients. We do not take legal fees.

We publish original content written and reviewed by people with deep knowledge of these subject areas.

Editorial Standards

All articles on this site are:

  • Written with reference to current Missouri and Illinois statutes
  • Reviewed for legal accuracy before publication
  • Updated when law or circumstances change
  • Clearly labeled as informational content, not legal advice

Disclaimer

Nothing on this website constitutes legal advice. No attorney-client relationship is created by reading or using this site. For advice about your specific situation, consult a licensed attorney in your state.

Contact

For editorial questions or to report inaccuracies: legal@rightswatch.com
